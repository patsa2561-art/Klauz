SERVICE AGREEMENT

1. Payment. The Client shall pay all invoices within 30 days of receipt.

2. Confidentiality. Each party must keep the other party's confidential information secret for a period of 5 years.

3. Liability. The Provider's total liability is capped at the total fees paid under this agreement.

4. Termination. Either party may terminate this agreement for cause with 60 days written notice.

5. Data. The Provider shall encrypt all customer data at rest and in transit.

6. Warranty. The warranty covers defects in workmanship for 12 months.

7. Governing Law. This agreement is governed by the laws of Singapore.
